Company History

Over two decades of excellence in ship management, delivering innovative solutions and maintaining the highest standards of maritime operations.

2000

Company Foundation

DORIKO LIMITED established in Seoul, Korea, as a specialized ship management company focused on technical excellence and maritime safety standards.

2002

First Major Project

Successfully supervised newbuilding project for chemical tanker M/T ASIAN GRACE, establishing our reputation for quality oversight and technical expertise in vessel construction.

2004

Conversion Expertise

Expanded capabilities with major vessel conversion projects, successfully transforming PCCs and bulk carriers into specialized vessels including cement carriers.

2005-08

Restoration Projects

Demonstrated expertise in complex vessel rehabilitation through successful restoration of fire and flood-damaged vessels, managing complete recovery operations.

2009

Fleet Expansion

Oversaw construction supervision of 8 × 13,000 DWT chemical tankers, significantly expanding our project management capabilities and technical expertise.

2015

Technology Innovation

Developed comprehensive "Vision SMS System" - an integrated ISM management system including PMS functionality, providing complete safety management solutions for enhanced operational efficiency.

2020-21

Fleet Diversification

Expanded operations to include second-hand bulk carrier and PCTC management with enhanced compliance programs and specialized technical services.

2023-24

Sustainability & Compliance

Implemented comprehensive ESG framework, developed customized PSC inspection programs, and established fleet-specific safety training protocols.

Present

Current Operations

Operating 14 vessels including PCTCs and bulk carriers, providing comprehensive full-cycle ship management solutions to global clients with multinational crew operations.
